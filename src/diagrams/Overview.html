Pairwise Interactions

Studying pairwise interactions surface some interaction effects, but they only show us two-dimensional slices of a space that has hundreds of dimensions and many of the combinations are not realistic.

Spatial Activations

Spatial activations show us important combinations of many neurons by sampling the sub-manifold of likely activations, but they are limited to those that occur in the given image example.

Activation Atlas

Activation atlases give us a bigger picture overview by sampling more of the manifold of likely activations.
